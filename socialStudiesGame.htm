EMPIRE SIM v. 0.1

Welcome to Empire Sim! You are the leader of your own growing empire, and your nation will come to you with questions. Advise them, and the decisions you make will determine the fate of your empire. The goal of the game is to make your empire last as many decisions as possible, as all empires will end eventually. Respond by clicking the response buttons at the bottom of the screen. Do not click next until you have chosen an answer. Click start when you are ready to begin. Good luck!
